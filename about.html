Who We Are


Pharmanect is Kenya's leading pharmacy discovery platform. We connect customers with verified pharmacies, making it easy to search, compare and order medicines online.


Our mission is to improve access to healthcare by leveraging technology and trusted pharmacy partnerships.

Our Mission

To make quality medicines accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted healthcare platform in Africa.

Our Values

Trust, Integrity, Innovation and Customer Care.
